Livestreaming is the real-time broadcasting of video or audio content over the internet, enabling viewers to watch and interact with events as they happen. Dyte offers livestreaming services to facilitates one-to-many communication, where a single source streams the content to multiple viewers or participants. It enables the hosts and participants to engage and interact during the stream, creating an interactive live streaming (ILS) experience.

<img
  src="/static/livestream/livestream.png"
  width="700"
  height="400"
  alt="livestream-overview"
  className="livestrem-hls-rtmp"
/>

On this page, you will find an overview to help you craft an exceptional user experience with minimal hassle. To kickstart your journey, the [Essentials](#essentials) section will guide you through the setup process and fundamental concepts. In the [Capabilities](#capabilities) section, you'll discover functionalities that you can incrementally add to enhance your livestream experience.

Dyte offers a prebuilt library of UI components that simplifies the integration of live streaming into any platform within minutes. In the [Prebuilt UI](#prebuilt-ui) section, you'll find guides on integrating them into your existing applications. There are also guides that can help you customise the components to align with your brand's identity.

Explore the [Reference](#reference) section to build a better understanding of the core SDK that provides simple APIs for complex media and networking optimizations driving your live streaming experience. Additionally, you can access references for the REST APIs that power your livestreams.

## Supported Platforms

Dyte's ILS is supported on the following platforms:

- Web
- Mobile
  - iOS
  - Android
  - Flutter
